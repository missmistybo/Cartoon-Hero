Jake the Dog

About Jake

Jake is the coolest! Jake is always down for a new adventure with his brother Finn and usually saves the day!

Characteristics

  1. Jake uses his Stretchy Powers to take any form.
  2. Jake is a very skilled musician, especially on the viola.
  3. Being a dog, Jake has an acute sense of smell - he can smell the precise location of an object miles away.
